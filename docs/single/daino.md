### Detailed Project Description 
DAIno is a chrome extension that adds a button to your social feed, allowing you to gift anyone xDAI. After selecting the DAIno button the sender deposits fund into their one time use DAIno address and weâ€™ll send the receiver a notification to let them know they got paid! The receiver can then claim the funds in using their social authentication on the DAIno page.
